## 🖼️ Profile Photo (Image Upload)

1. Go to admin panel `/admin/about`
2. Click on the upload area
3. Select a professional photo (JPG, PNG, or GIF)
4. File should be under 5MB
5. Best aspect ratio: Square (1:1) or slightly wider (4:3)

---

## 📋 Format Tips

### Education Format

```
[Degree Name]
[School/University] | [Year Range]
[Optional details about the program]

[Next Degree]
[School/University] | [Year Range]
[Optional details]
```

### Experience Format

```
[Job Title]
[Company Name] | [Year Range]
[What you did/Responsibilities]

[Next Job]
[Company Name] | [Year Range]
[What you did]
```

### Tips

- ✅ Write naturally - no special formatting needed
- ✅ Separate entries with blank lines
- ✅ Be specific with years (e.g., "2023 - Present" or "2021 - 2023")
- ✅ Add company/school information on the second line
- ✅ Keep descriptions concise but meaningful
- ✅ You can update anytime!

---

## 🚀 How to Add This Data

1. Go to `http://localhost:3000/admin/about`
2. Fill in each field:
   - **Profile Photo**: Click to upload
   - **About You**: Paste professional summary
   - **Education Details**: Paste education history
   - **Experience Details**: Paste work experience
   - **Resume Link**: Enter your resume URL
3. Click **"Save About Section"** button
4. Visit `http://localhost:3000/about` to see your beautiful timeline!
